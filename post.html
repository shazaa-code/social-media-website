Infinite Jest — David Foster Wallace

How far I got 62% through
I made it to page 500. That's 500 pages of genuinely brilliant, occasionally maddening prose. The footnotes had become their own novel — I was reading two books simultaneously, neither of which seemed interested in resolving anytime soon. At some point during week three I put it down to make dinner and just… didn't pick it back up. Not out of dislike — the writing is stunning in places. But the sheer weight of it started to feel less like reading and more like a relationship I wasn't sure I'd consented to. Maybe I'll return. But honestly? I think I'm okay with the 62% I got.
